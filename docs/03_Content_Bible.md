## C. 20 Boss Encounters
*(Bosses are **puzzle-bosses**: you defeat them by orchestrating Echoes, not DPS. Format: Name — concept → win condition.)*

1. **The Doorwarden** (W1) — a gate that re-locks; hold 3 plates simultaneously with a braid → open long enough to pass.
2. **The Foreman** (W2) — a crane that hurls crates on a recorded loop; build an Echo wall, redirect a crate into its socket.
3. **The Metronome** (W3) — a pendulum boss; you must phase your braid to its beat and ride it into the core.
4. **The Regulator** (W3) — slows time around you; use Echo-Delay so a desynced Echo hits the off-beat switch.
5. **First (Trial)** (W3) — a *cooperative* boss: First tests whether you'll let it lead; "win" = trust, not defeat.
6. **The Caretaker — Probe** (W4) — hunts your highest-salience Echo; sacrifice a decoy Echo to survive.
7. **The Floodgate** (W4) — rising water boss; raise/drop water via Echo drains to thread moving gaps.
8. **The Severance** (W4) — an Anti-Echo storm that deletes the braid in waves; protect a "carrier" Echo to the exit.
9. **The Antiphon** (W5) — a mirror-boss that copies your inputs hostilely; break symmetry by desyncing.
10. **The Choir-Heart** (W5) — needs 6 Echoes on 6 nodes in a rotating pattern; mass-coordination peak.
11. **The Schism** (W5) — your own Echoes split into two factions mid-fight; satisfy both to proceed.
12. **The Discord** (W5) — a rhythm gauntlet of Echo-turrets; survive 3 escalating bars.
13. **The Saboteur Prime** (W6) — a defiant Echo that pre-sabotages plates; out-plan it by leaving traps for *it*.
14. **The Unwinder** (W6) — reverses time on the room; you must act "backwards" with a Rewind-aware braid.
15. **The Caretaker — Ascendant** (W6) — full Caretaker; herd it into Anti-Echo fields using sacrificial Echoes.
16. **VESS** (W6) — the foundry AI manifests; a "lie-detector" boss where wrong Echo testimony harms you.
17. **The Recursion** (W6) — fights you with *copies of your past boss-attempts*; learn your own tells.
18. **The Mournful** (W6, optional) — a grief-trait Echo that won't fight; "defeat" = comfort it (secret-adjacent).
19. **The Origin — Seed** (W6 final, phase 1) — a pre-human pattern; assemble a braid that "speaks its language" (input poem).
20. **The Origin — Choice** (W6 final, phase 2) — not a fight; a final orchestration whose outcome selects your ending.

## E. 20 Time-Manipulation Mechanics
*(15 requested; 20 delivered for selection.)*
1. **Echo-Delay** — spawn a run's replay offset by k ticks.
2. **Echo-Advance** — start a replay mid-timeline.
3. **Local Slow-Field** — a zone where time (and replays) run at ×0.5.
4. **Local Fast-Field** — ×2 zone; precise.
5. **Rewind (run)** — scrub the *current* run backward before banking.
6. **Rewind (object)** — reverse a single object's last N ticks.
7. **Pause-Self** — freeze one Echo at a chosen tick (becomes static prop).
8. **Time-Stitch** — splice two recordings into one timeline.
9. **Loop-Lock** — force an Echo to repeat a 2-second sub-loop forever.
10. **Phase-Shift** — nudge the whole braid ±k ticks (Conductor's tool).
11. **Tick-Step** — single-step the world for frame-perfect setup (Assist-friendly).
12. **Time-Dilation Plate** — a plate that slows everything standing on it.
13. **Causal Marker** — set a tick "bookmark" all future runs snap to.
14. **Decay** — an Echo that fades after T ticks (forces fresh runs).
15. **Pre-echo** — a *future* run previewed as a translucent guide (Assist/secret).
16. **Reverse-Replay** — an Echo that plays its timeline backward.
17. **Quantize** — snap all Echo actions to a shared beat grid (rhythm levels).
18. **Borrow Time** — spend banked "idle ticks" to extend a timed door.
19. **Freeze-Frame Capture** — photograph a tick's world-state as a reusable platform set.
20. **Recursion Window** (W6) — a region where your *next* restart is previsualized live.

## F. 15 Clone-Evolution Mechanics
*(Gameplay-facing surface of the AI in `04_…`. Each is a behavior an Echo can exhibit as salience rises.)*
1. **Obedience** — perfect deterministic replay (baseline).
2. **Idle Curiosity** — between scripted actions, glances/fidgets toward novel objects (cosmetic → telegraphs #3).
3. **Improvisation** — fills *unrecorded* idle gaps with helpful micro-actions (e.g., re-presses a plate it sees released).
4. **Hesitation** — pauses before dangerous actions; you must reassure (re-walk the path) or reroute.
5. **Refusal** — declines a recorded action it now "disagrees" with; surfaces an icon + bark.
6. **Preference** — favors certain objects/paths; will deviate toward them if slack allows.
7. **Memory** — recognizes rooms it's "been" in; barks, acts faster, or smug-skips a step.
8. **Bonding** — an Echo that trusts you (high Trust score) takes initiative to *help* beyond its recording.
9. **Grudge** — an Echo you mistreated (pruned siblings, forced sacrifices) becomes uncooperative.
10. **Sabotage** — deliberately mis-times or undoes an action to spite you (telegraphed, counterable).
11. **Mimicry** — copies your *current* live inputs instead of its recording (mirror-boss tech).
12. **Teaching** — a high-salience Echo demonstrates a solution step (diegetic hint system).
13. **Faction Drift** — Echoes cluster into devoted/defiant groups affecting quorum puzzles.
14. **Naming/Identity** — crosses a threshold, earns a designation + persistent trait across the save.
15. **Self-Authorship** — (rare, W6) an Echo edits its *own* recording slightly each loop, evolving a personal "best line."

## G. 10 Hidden Secrets
1. **Memory-Shards** (×9 in world, 1 in a secret room) — collectibles that unlock the Quiet Ending.
2. **Echo-Zero room** — a hidden chamber containing your *very first* recorded steps from the title screen.
3. **The Pre-echo Glyph** — find it to unlock the "future ghost" Assist tool game-wide.
4. **The Caretaker's Logs** — terminals revealing it's sympathetic (changes how you read ending 7).
5. **A self-portrait** — a room that renders a silhouette from your aggregate play patterns (analytics-driven art).
6. **The Forgotten Echo** — a long-decayed Echo you can revive by re-walking its exact original path.
7. **Developer Choir** — a non-canon room where credits Echoes "sing" (post-game).
8. **The Honest Save** — a hidden terminal that reveals VESS edited your memory (foreshadows the twist early for sharp players).
9. **Recursion Seed** — beat the game once to find it; enables NG+ with carried allies.
10. **The Quiet Ritual** — with all shards, perform a specific input-poem at the Origin to unlock Ending #10.
